Crown & Bridge

Precision‑milled crowns and bridges fabricated with advanced CAD/CAM technology for long‑lasting strength and a natural, lifelike finish. Every restoration is quality‑checked to ensure a predictable fit, saving chair‑time and enhancing patient satisfaction.

Zirconia

High‑strength monolithic and layered zirconia for posterior and anterior cases, balancing durability with exceptional translucency.

Hybrid Ceramics

Polymer‑infiltrated ceramic network (PICN) restorations that absorb masticatory stress and protect opposing dentition.

Glass Ceramics

Lithium‑disilicate options ideal for highly esthetic single crowns, inlays, onlays, and veneers.

Temporary Restoration

Digitally‑designed PMMA provisionals that safeguard preparations and maintain occlusion while definitive work is in progress.

Digital Precious Metal

Laser‑sintered cobalt‑chromium and selective gold restorations for cases that require proven longevity and biocompatibility.

Guided Implant Surgery

End‑to‑end digital planning services and surgical guides that streamline implant placement, increase accuracy, and enhance restorative outcomes.

Implant Planning Service

CBCT‑based virtual planning with restorative‑driven positioning and interactive case reviews.

Surgical Guide Production

High‑precision 3‑D printed guides verified for fit and sterilizability before delivery.

Implant Restorations

Comprehensive restorative options for all major implant systems, engineered to achieve optimal emergence profiles and long‑term soft‑tissue stability.

Abutments

Custom titanium and zirconia abutments milled to precise connections and ideal gingival contours.

Screw‑Retained

Integrated crown‑abutment solutions that simplify placement and facilitate retrievability.

Overdenture

Durable bar and locator‑based overdenture frameworks designed for stability and patient comfort.

Prosthetics

Our high-quality prosthetic solutions are fabricated with precision and care, offering both functionality and natural esthetics. Whether your case requires full or partial dentures, we use state-of-the-art materials and CAD/CAM workflows to ensure patient satisfaction and long-term performance.

Full Dentures

Crafted for comfort, fit, and durability, our full dentures restore complete arches with a natural look and feel. Each set is designed to meet individual anatomy and occlusion needs using pressure-mapped and 3D printed techniques for superior results.

Partial Dentures

Lightweight and highly esthetic, our partial dentures provide a reliable and removable solution for patients with remaining natural dentition. Available in flexible, acrylic, or metal frameworks to match both clinical requirements and patient preferences.
